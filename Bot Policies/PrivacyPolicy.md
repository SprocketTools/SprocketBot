# Privacy Policy
Last updated June 12th, 2025

This privacy policy applies to all variants of Sprocket Bot hosted officially, including Scamsatsu.
## What Sprocket Bot will do:
- As Sprocket Bot is integrated around scam prevention measures, Sprocket Bot processes all user content that it has access to.  Information is only saved or collected when it detects user content to be malicious.
- Specific messages will also be made visible to the bot developer(s) if they are detected to be malicious.  This is to ensure that the bot's services can be improved over time to better serve its users.
- Sprocket Bot can save artwork and in-game creations for later use.
	- As an example, Sprocket Bot will save images that users submit for the SprocketTools decal catalog, or save blueprints for a user-hosted contest.

## What Sprocket Bot will never do:
- Sprocket Bot will never sell your information to third party developers.
- Sprocket Bot will never collect artwork, in-game creations, or similar content and redistribute it as its own.
